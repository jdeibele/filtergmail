What the file does
✓
Junk you list gets a “From Spam” or “Subject Spam” label and goes to Trash (kept 30 days, just in case).
✓
Mail you label — like everything from your bank — keeps its spot in your inbox and just gets your label too.
✓
It all applies to new mail from now on; your existing inbox is never touched.
Import it, once
1
In the Gmail website, click the gear (⚙) → “See all settings”.
2
Open the “Filters and Blocked Addresses” tab.
3
Scroll down, choose “Import filters,” and pick the file you downloaded.
4
Click “Create filters.” Done — new mail is handled automatically.
Use the Gmail website, not the app. On a phone you can open Gmail in Safari and tap “Desktop site,” but it's easier on a computer.
